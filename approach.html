How We Build

We found, finance,
and operate.

Two River is a venture-creation firm and merchant bank for the life sciences. We invest our own capital and build companies hands-on, from the first idea to the public markets.

The model

A unique partnership

Found

We co-create companies with scientific founders — shaping the thesis, the intellectual property, and the team from day one.

Finance

We invest our own capital and assemble the syndicates that carry a company through the clinic and into the public markets.

Operate

Corporate strategy, business development, and day-to-day operating support. Each investment is a true partnership.

Lead

We recruit world-class management and mentor it — from the first hire to the public-company boardroom.
