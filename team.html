Two River Group Holdings, LLC was founded in September 2004 by Joshua Kazam, Peter Kash, David Tanen, and Benjamin Bernstein. Dr. Arie Belldegrun joined as Chairman & Partner in 2008. The firm's partners have co-founded and built companies including Kite Pharma, Allogene Therapeutics, Kronos Bio, Candid Therapeutics, and Neogene Therapeutics.
