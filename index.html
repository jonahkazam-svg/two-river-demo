The Portfolio

Built by Two River.

Companies founded, financed, and backed by Two River — across cancer, immunology, and the infrastructure of biotech.

Kite Pharma

Gilead · $11.9B

Co-founded by Two River. Delivered Yescarta™ — a first FDA-approved CAR T — and was acquired by Gilead Sciences in 2017 for $11.9 billion.

Allogene Therapeutics

Public · ALLO

Off-the-shelf allogeneic CAR T for blood cancers, solid tumors, and autoimmune disease. Co-founded by Two River.
